The Issue

Bee hives only have a 5% spawn chance throughout the Minecraft world under the right conditions. This is no where near enough space for a healthy species of bee. Every year tens of bees are dying due to this lack of homes. Without bees, flowers would have very little pollination. It is evident that something must be done to preserve the ecosystem.
