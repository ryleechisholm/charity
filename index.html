Homes for Honeybees

In Minecraft

Bee hives only have a 5% spawn chance throughout the Minecraft world under the right conditions. This is no where near enough space for a healthy species of bee. Every year tens of bees are dying due to this lack of homes. Without bees, flowers would have very little pollination.

For just 1 diamond a week, my crew will craft hives for the honeybees, as well as planting plenty of flowers to help populate the honeybee species. Together, we will make a prettier and bee-filled Minecraft world.
